About Amey

Amey Lole founded Tech With Amey under the Gospel Grace Education initiative to make industry-aligned coding education accessible without the cost or rigidity of a traditional bootcamp. He works as a full-stack developer and enterprise consultant alongside running the program, which keeps the curriculum grounded in tools and patterns actually used on commercial projects rather than theory alone.

Over the past two-plus years, that approach has carried the program to more than 10,000 learners across 30-plus countries, and into more than 15 commercial collaborations with partner businesses — work that now feeds directly back into the live internship track, giving learners a path from first lesson to real client work.
